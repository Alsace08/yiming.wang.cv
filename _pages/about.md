**Hello, I'm Yiming Wang.**

I was born in Jiangsu Province, China, in August 2001. I'm now a first-year Ph.D. candidate at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University(SJTU), supervised by Prof. Rui Wang. Before that, I received a Bachelor's degree from the Institute of Artificial Intelligence, Beihang University(BUAA).

I mainly focus on these areas oriented towards Large Language Models (LLMs) and Machine Learning (ML):

* Complex Reasoning **(Primary)**:

  - Pushing the Upper Limits of LLMs' Known Reasoning Ability [Meta-Reasoning, ACL 2024]

  - Exploring the Boundaries of LLM Reasoning Ability [OOD Detection in MATH, Arxiv 2024]


* Discrete Generation:

  - Text Generation: Summarization [SumCoT, ACL 2023]; [CPSUM, COLING 2022]

  - Molecular Generation: Retrosynthesis [RetroDiff, Arxiv 2023]

* Others: LLM Agent, Computational Linguistics
